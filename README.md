# Regression Interpretation, Classification Metrics & Advertising Prediction

**Tools:** Python · scikit-learn · Linear Regression · Logistic Regression · Classification Metrics · pandas

---

## Problem

Business analysts needed to interpret regression coefficients in real-world terms, evaluate classification model performance beyond simple accuracy, and predict sales outcomes from marketing spend data.

## Approach

Applied linear and logistic regression to real datasets including used car pricing and bank marketing data. Interpreted exponentiated coefficients to quantify the impact of individual variables on price and subscription odds. Computed and interpreted a full confusion matrix including accuracy, precision, recall, and specificity. Built a simple linear regression model to predict sales from Facebook advertising budget.

## Impact

Demonstrated that a one-year increase in model year is associated with a 12.5% increase in selling price, and that Facebook ad spend explains approximately 94.6% of variation in sales (R-squared = 0.946), providing actionable insight for pricing strategy and marketing budget allocation.
